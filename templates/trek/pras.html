PRASHAR LAKE

PRASHAR LAKE-THE EPITOME OF BEAUTY

Considered amongst the best treks within the country, the Kedarkantha trek have risen into the most loved expedition where one finds fun and jaw-dropping views all the way through. Just covering 23kms ,it is seemed as the easiest snow peak summit and a starter trek for the wayfarers who desire to go for mega-expeditions in the times to come. For all the snow lovers, this is a perfect trek to enjoy and capture within hearts and cameras, the snow caped statuesque beauty of the nature. Sankri and Kotgaon, the base camps for this summit, are perfect hosts to give the trekkers and visitors a real touch and taste of the local folk culture.

THE BEAUTY OF KEDARKANTHA AS WELL AS THE PATH THAT LEADS TO THIS MESMERISING PLACE WILL DEFINITELY AMAZE YOU

SHORT ITINERARY of KEDARKANTHA TREK

Day 1: Arrive at Kotgaon and & Acclimatize.

:        10 hrs drive from Dehradun

:        CLIFFCAT shops available for hiring and buying trekking gears .

Day 2: Kotgaon to Juda ka Talab(6 hrs trek)

Day 3: Juda ka Talab to KEDARKANTHA PEAK and back to Juda ka Talab(4 hrs trek up ,Expert brief ,exploring and 2hrs trek down)

Day 4:  Juda ka Talab to Kotgaon.(4 hrs trek)

Day 5: Departure from Kotgaon.
